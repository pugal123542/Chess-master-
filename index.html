Game Rules
Objective

Checkmate your opponent's King — place it under attack with no legal escape.

Pieces

♙ Pawn — 1 forward (2 first move). Captures diagonally. Promotes on last rank.

♖ Rook — Horizontal/vertical, unlimited.

♘ Knight — L-shape (2+1). Jumps over pieces.

♗ Bishop — Diagonal, unlimited.

♕ Queen — Any direction, unlimited.

♔ King — 1 square any direction.

Special Rules

Castling — King + Rook positional swap (neither moved, no pieces between, King not in check).

En Passant — Pawn captures pawn that just advanced 2 squares beside it.

Promotion — Pawn reaching last rank promotes to Queen, Rook, Bishop or Knight.
